My Love for You, Aru

From the very first moment we started talking, I knew there was something different about you, something pure, something real. Tumhari baaton mein ek aisi khushi thi jo dil ko chhoo jaati thi, aur aaj bhi har baar tumse baat karta hoon, dil wahi sukoon mehsoos karta hai.

Aru, main shayad likhne mein perfect na hoon, lekin jo mehsoos karta hoon woh har lafz mein utarna chahta hoon. You're not just a part of my day — you're the reason it becomes beautiful. When I wake up, the first thought is of your smile. When I sleep, the last feeling is your warmth.

Tumhari awaaz, tumhara hansna, tumhari shararat bhari baatein — sab kuch itna apna lagta hai. Jab tum thoda sa gussa karti ho na, woh bhi mujhe cute lagta hai. Mujhe lagta hai, duniya mein koi aur tumhari jagah nahi le sakta, aur lena bhi nahi chahiye.

We’ve shared little fights, endless calls, random late-night confessions, aur woh chhoti-chhoti baatein jo sirf hum dono ke beech hoti hain. Kabhi kabhi lagta hai — kaise possible hai kisi se itna deeply connect kar paana? Lekin tum ho, toh sab possible lagta hai.

Main janta hoon, har relation mein challenges hote hain. Aur humne bhi woh sab feel kiya hai. But we always come back — kyunki humara bond sirf love ka nahi, respect, patience aur care ka bhi hai. Tum meri life ka woh hissa ho jo har version mein important hai — khushiyon mein, lows mein, aur har beech wale pal mein.

Fuggan, kabhi kabhi sochta hoon — agar tum nahi hoti, toh main kaise hota? Shayad ek blank page, jisme koi kahani nahi likhi gayi. Tum aayi aur meri zindagi ko ek kahani de gayi — har din nayi line, har pal naya emotion.

I love how naturally you became my home. Tumse baat kiye bina din adhura lagta hai. Aur jab tum “I love you” bolti ho na, toh lagta hai duniya ki sabse best feeling mujhe mil gayi.

Jab main kehata hoon “I love you”, usmein sirf teen shabd nahi hote. Usmein meri puri duniya hoti hai. Tumhare liye mera pyaar kisi poem ki tarah nahi, ek silent prayer ki tarah hai — har roz, har waqt, tumhare saath ke liye.

Main tumse perfect love story ka wada nahi karta. But main yeh zaroor kehta hoon — jitna ho sake, main tumhare saath rahunga. Tumhara support banunga, tumhari khushi mein khud ki khushi dhoondhunga.

Let's keep walking together, Aru — bina kisi fear ke, bina kisi pressure ke, sirf pyaar aur trust ke saath. Chahe jitne bhi ups and downs aayein, main tumhara haath nahi chhodunga.

Tum ho meri zindagi ki sabse khoobsurat feeling. Tumhari aankhon mein jo sapne hain, main unhe sach karna chahta hoon. Tumhare saath har subah, har shaam, ek nayi kahani likhni hai.

Bas itna hi kehna chahta hoon — I’m proud to be yours. I’m blessed that you’re mine. Aur har din tumse aur zyada pyaar hota ja raha hai.

Love you forever,
